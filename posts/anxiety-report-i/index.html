Anxiety Report: Week I

2 minutes read

Workplace anxiety

My workplace is where I experience the most of my anxiety, this sort of panic attacks happened in the following situations:

My anxiety symptoms are the following

  • I feel like I can’t breathe
  • I’m unable to swallow
  • I experience a lot of fear to die by choking

On the way through the Hall

When I arrive early in the morning, most of the times I’m unable to move. I need to wait one of my colleagues for recover and being able to move again.

When I need to go to the bathroom

I simply cannot breathe, I also got this huge fear that increase at every step.

At lunch time on the way to the canteen

I experience same feelings I described above, in particular here I usually have a strong feeling of being unable to swallow. I can’t free my mind from evil toughts and same as morning mostly of the times I’m unable to move.

During lunch time

I always had a lot of fear of choking when having lunch away from home

Anxiety free moment

My best anxiety free moment of the week was on Thursday. I was able to go through the all without having any symptoms.

This needs to be remembered because it didn’t happend very often.
